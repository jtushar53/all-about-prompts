# What is a Prompt?

A **prompt** is simply the text you type to an AI to tell it what you want. It's like giving instructions to a very smart, very fast helper.

When you open a chat with an AI like ChatGPT or Claude and type "Tell me a joke," that sentence is your prompt. The AI reads it and figures out what you're asking for.

## Why prompts matter

The AI doesn't know what's in your head. It only knows what you write. A clear, detailed prompt gets a great answer. A vague prompt gets a vague answer.

Think of it like ordering food:

- **Vague**: "Give me something to eat."
- **Clear**: "I'd like a vegetarian burrito with rice, beans, cheese, and salsa."

The second order is much more likely to get you exactly what you want!

## Your first prompt

Try typing this to any AI:

```
Write a short poem about a dog who loves pizza.
```

Notice how specific it is — we said *short*, we said *poem*, we said *dog*, and we said *pizza*. Each word shapes the answer.
